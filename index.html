F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Where are you located?

    Our clinic is located on the Ground Floor at Radhika Apartment, 10th Road, Near Cream Center, Behind Cult Fit Gym, JVPD Scheme, Juhu, Mumbai.

  • How do I make an appointment?

    Simply give us a call at +91 9698 509 509 or visit us at our clinic in Juhu, Mumbai. Schedule your appointment today and transform your dental care experience with DentSure!

  • What dental services do you provide?

    DentSure is a multispecialty dental clinic with a comprehensive range of dental services from preventive care to specialised treatment. Our services include preventive dentistry, restorative dentistry, cosmetic dentistry, teeth whitening, oral surgery, sedation dentistry, full mouth reconstruction, and Invisalign.

  • Is there parking available near your clinic?

    Yes, on-street parking is located in front of the building depending on availability.

  • Do you accept walk-ins?

    Yes, walk-ins are welcome! Kindly check with the front desk about same day services and confirm appointment availability.

  • Should I bring anything with me to my consultation?

    Yes. If you are visiting for a follow-up, please bring your DentSure file with you.

  • How often should I visit the dentist?

    We recommend appointments once every 6 months for routine dental checkups and cleanings. These help us identify any problems before they become serious and keep your oral health in check.
